6 Best Places to Visit in Georgia This November

1

BORJOMI - AXALTSIKHE

Journey to the majestic mountains of the Lesser Caucasus, where the IX-century Rabat Castle stands tall. Discover Mtskheta, a 2,400-year-old city, home to the UNESCO World Heritage Jvari Monastery. Rejuvenate with the warm, healing waters of Borjomi, rich with 12 minerals, flowing from 1,500 meters. Explore the vast wilderness of Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park and savor Georgian cuisine crafted from centuries-old recipes, accompanied by wines made from endemic grapes using 8,000-year-old traditions
